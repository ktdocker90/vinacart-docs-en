Vinacart development theme
==========================
Theme guide: `codex.vinacart.net <http://codex.vinacart.net>`_

This is a guide to VinaCart Ecommerce theme design for users and developers. This document covers many aspects related to setting up your store, as well as user interface development guidelines.

Documentation guides you through the parts of the management interface and provides solutions and hints to most of your ecommerce functional areas. You will be able to understand how VinaCart works, how you can modify and set up the blocks, the store interface to customize according to your needs.

After reading this guide, you can easily use the VinaCart platform to set up your online store and sell your way. Here is a table of contents so that you can begin to familiarize and effectively use VinaCart.
